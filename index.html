REDEFINING LEARNING

Welcome to Whyena, a technology-based startup charting a new path for education. We are a team of educators and developers, all working towards a common goal of changing to way we define teaching and learning. We believe that Whyena has the potential to promote a transfer of responsibility in learning from teacher to student through empowering learners to be proactive in their learning using technology.
